About Daystar Hockey Club

The Daystar Hockey Club is a community of passionate players committed to growing the sport of field hockey at Daystar University and beyond. As part of the university's athletics program, our team competes in regional and national tournaments.

Our Mission

To foster teamwork, discipline, and excellence on and off the field, while inspiring the next generation of hockey players.
